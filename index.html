Our Course

Welcome to this Chinese Idioms course, in this course we will learn 100 most commonly used Chinese idioms through a quiz and tracking your process

Requirements

Prefebably the students have taken Mandarin Chinese in college or has a begginer level and students that are interested in Chinese language and culture.

Description

Chinese idioms were originated from the ancient Chinese historical and philosophical works, crystallizing the wisdom of Chinese culture through thousands of years. They have become familiar daily language of today spoken by ordinary Chinese people in daily life.

Who is this course for

Aimed for students who are interested in learning Chinese language or that are interested in Chinese culture, especially the four major schools of classical philosophy.

About

I am a student in La Salle Girona who has a big passion for the Chinese language and its culture. As a Chinese born overseas, I am not fluent and lack vocabulary, specially on Chinese Idioms, which are used daily. With a little research, I found out that most of the Chinese people like me also struggle with Idioms, so I decided to make a small program to learn the most used Chinese Idioms.
